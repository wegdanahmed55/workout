Dumbbell curl is also known as Biceps Curl. It is one of the most popular moves that comes to mind when you think of arm training.
Dumbbell curl is a basic strength training exercise to build muscle and strength in the arms. Specifically, the dumbbell curl works all the muscles in the front of the arm. It is a highly effective exercise for maximize the bicep peak. Additionally, you can use this movement to correct symmetry issues. (for example, if one bicep is bigger than another)
