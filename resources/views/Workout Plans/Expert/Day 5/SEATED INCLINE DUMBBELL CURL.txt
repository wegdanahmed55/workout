Incline dumbbell curl is an isolation exercise that works the long head of your biceps, stretches it and helps it in contracting with more force during the exercise. If your goal is to get stronger and bigger arms, you can add incline dumbbell curls to your exercise routine
The primary target of incline dumbbell curl movement is the biceps brachii muscle. Additionally, you can use this movement to correct symmetry issues. (for example, if one bicep is bigger than another)
